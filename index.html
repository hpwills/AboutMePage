I graduated from Hewitt-Trussville High School in 2013. I attended the University of Alabama at Birmingham for 2 years before moving to Los Angeles, California. During my time in Los Angeles, I gained experience in many different fields. I was on the managerial team at Six Flags Magic Mountain. After my employment with Six Flags, I began working with GEICO as an auto damage adjuster around the time when the COVID-19 pandemic began. After a short stint at GEICO I found myself, like many others across the world, unemployed for a brief period of time before beginning work at Loomis Armored. At Loomis I was a Vault Technician as well as an Armored Service Technician and Driver. I handled large amounts of money and ensured that they reached their destination safely. After working for Loomis, I was employed with the City of Glendale as a Community Service Officer at the police department. After working for the City of Glendale for approximately 6 months, I was rehired at Loomis, and transferred to the Chattanooga branch in Tennessee where I am currently employed. I have gained experience in an abundance of different job areas and I have learned to adapt and to learn quickly, but I know that my dream is to work in Web Development. What I truly want is to design engaging, meaningful, user-friendly, appealing websites and applications. The more I learn about coding, the more fascinated I become. I have always had a passion for technology and I could not have made a better decision than choosing to pursue a career in Web Development. I am currently enrolled in online courses and have acquired multiple certifications in Frontend Web Development including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am eager to learn and I look forward to all of the things I will create.
